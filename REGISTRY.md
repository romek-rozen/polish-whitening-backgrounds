## How to use

Pick a background by `name`, point siteFocus at its `path`:

```bash
.venv/bin/python -m src.sitefocus.cli.run_full \
    --in  <run_dir> \
    --out <sf_dir> \
    --load-whitening models/sitefocus_backgrounds/<name>
```
